Commissioning Process

If you want to ask me to make something for you, here's how you can reach out.
Step 1: Go to the socials tab in my portfolio. Step 2: Contact me via one of the platforms listed. Step 3: Let me know who you are, what you want me to create, and things like a deadline and things you want me to focus on. Step 4: We'll discuss the details such as pricing and project coordination.

Pricing

Small projects: Things like basic GUI work, basic map generation, NPC systems, debugging, or reworking parts of your game (this depends on complexity).



Medium projects: Projects like inventory systems, basic quests, or medium-level features for your game.



Large projects: More complex things like combat systems, combat AI, movement mechanics, or anything that's complex and takes a lot of time to complete.

Extra Info

I don't charge per hour because that would mean I would deliver your work quicker for less money. I just charge based on the size of the project itself; we discuss this during the project and before we start working together.

If you have a passion project and you are in need of a scripter, I am willing to work for free with up to three people, depending on the amount of work you want me to do. This is a limited offer because, right now, I value gaining experience a lot more, and I genuinely enjoy creating projects together with people! Even if your project feels too large, please don't hesitate to ask. I'm open to discussing it because if I like the project myself, you'd be surprised by what I'm willing to do for you! And in case I do ask for money but I just like your project idea, there will be a price lowering for you.
